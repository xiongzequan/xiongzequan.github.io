Biography

Zequan Xiong is a Research Librarian and Master's Supervisor at East China Normal University. He serves as the Director of the Interdisciplinary Support Center at ECNU Library and Deputy Director of the Information Research Institute. With over 30 publications in CSSCI and SSCI journals, his research focuses on scientometrics and digital economy.
